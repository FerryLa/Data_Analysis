### Cost Structure Analysis

| Component | 2021 | 2024 | Interpretation |
|-----------|------|------|----------------|
| D&A / CoR | 49% | 31% | Hardware cost share **declining** |
| Other CoR / CoR | 51% | 69% | Data operations cost share **increasing** |
| CoR / Revenue | 43% | 64% | Overall cost intensity **rising** (scaling phase) |

### Key Insights

1. **2022 Margin Collapse (56.8% → 24.2%)**
   - Rapid satellite constellation expansion
   - D&A doubled ($9.2M → $18.3M)
   - Gross margin compressed during infrastructure build

2. **2023-2024 Stabilization**
   - D&A growth slowed while revenue accelerated
   - Operating leverage beginning to materialize
   - D&A/Revenue improved from 25.9% to 19.7%

3. **Cost Structure Transition**
   - D&A share of CoR: 49% → 31% (hardware → data shift)
   - Operations costs growing faster than depreciation
   - Typical pattern for maturing space data companies

4. **2024 Strategic Pivot**
   - Maritime business divestiture ($241M sale to Kpler)
   - Focus shifting to Weather, Aviation, Space Services
   - Moving from "build satellites" to "sell data insights"
